LGBT Equal Employment: What's your policy?

Let's require some civil rights from employers and events we help promote.

Required:

  • equal employment protections for LGBT employees
  • no benefits discrimination against LGBT employees, their partners, and their dependents

Additional information from the Human Rights Campaign's Corporate Equality Index

1. Equal employment opportunity policy includes:
  • a. Sexual orientation
  • b. Gender identity or expression
