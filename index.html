Tech Lady Hackathon + Training Day DC #3 is August 8

It will be at ImpactHub DC, near all metro lines. Subscribe to the newsletter to be the first to hear details (and learn about other tech lady news, events, and training every month).

Coders, tech strategists, trainers, newbies, and ideas are welcome! Not sure what to expect/bring/do? What to expect at a civic hackathon.

Over 150 women attended the 2nd Tech Lady Hackathon: The Second Tech Lady Hackathon was Tremendous by Shannon Turner.

About

Training: We offer training and mentoring workshops throughout the day - no prior knowledge of code is necessary. And we mean none. Some examples of classes are: Intro to... Ruby, APIs, Python, GitHub, html/CSS, and SQL.

Hackathon: This is a civic hackathon, and we usually work on projects from Code for DC, but you're welcome to bring anything and pitch it.
