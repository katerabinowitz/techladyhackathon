About

We started this to get more women into tech. Coders, tech strategists, trainers, newbies, nerds, and the curious are all welcome! At the Tech Lady Hackathon + Training Day, hundreds of women come together for a fun day of friendly, supportive learning, hacking, and networking.

What to expect at the event

Here's what we offer at the Tech Lady Hackathon + Training Days:

Training: We offer training and mentoring workshops throughout the day - no prior knowledge of code is necessary. And we mean none. Some examples of classes are: Intro to... Ruby, APIs, Python, GitHub, html/CSS, and SQL.

Hackathon: This is a civic hackathon, and we usually work on projects from Code for DC, but you're welcome to bring anything and pitch it.

New to hackathons? Not sure what to expect, bring, or do? Check out this post: What to expect at a hackathon.

Questions? Ideas? Advice?

Leah Bannon is the lead organizer of this event. Please feel free to reach out via Twitter with any questions or ideas, or you can check out her personal site at Leah.io.
