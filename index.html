About the Hackathon and Training

Training:
We offer basic 101 intro training and mentoring workshops throughout the day - no prior knowledge of code is necessary. And we mean none.

Some examples of the 101 intro classes we offer are: Ruby, APIs, Python, GitHub, html/CSS, and SQL. Mentoring sessions we offered previously were: Empowerment/imposter syndrome, open gov/open data, and lady mentoring session.

Hackathon:
This is a civic hackathon, and we usually work on projects from Code for DC, but you're welcome to bring anything.
